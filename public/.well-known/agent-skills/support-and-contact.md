# Pack Support And Contact

Use this skill when an agent needs to route a user to Pack support or identify the right public support surface.

## Primary sources

- Support page: `https://www.trypackai.com/support`
- Support email: `mailto:support@trypackai.com`
- Privacy request page: `https://www.trypackai.com/privacy-request`

## Guidance

1. Send product and account-support questions to the Support page first.
2. Use `support@trypackai.com` when a direct email contact is needed.
3. Send privacy and data-rights requests to the privacy-request flow instead of general support whenever possible.
