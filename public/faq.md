# Pack FAQ

## What is Pack?

Pack is an AI travel app for organizing a trip when the details are spread across prompts, confirmations, calendars, and other tools.

## Is Pack an AI travel planner?

Yes. Pack is positioned as an AI travel planner that helps assemble a trip draft before booking.

## What problem does it solve?

It reduces research sprawl and trip-management fragmentation by giving travelers one connected view of the trip.
