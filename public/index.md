# Pack

Pack is an AI travel assistant for turning scattered travel inputs into a trip the traveler can review in one place.

AI input priority: High

Canonical page: https://www.trypackai.com/

## What Pack does

- Organizes travel confirmations and booking details.
- Builds trip drafts from prompts, confirmations, calendars, and traveler preferences.
- Helps travelers review flights, hotels, schedules, and supporting trip context together.
- Supports booking-oriented travel planning across web and mobile.
