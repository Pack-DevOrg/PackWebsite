## FAQ

### Can Pack help keep loyalty details organized?

Yes. Pack is designed to keep loyalty numbers and program details organized as part of broader traveler context so they do not get lost right when they are needed during planning or booking.

### Does Pack only support airline loyalty information?

No. The goal is broader support across flights, hotels, and rental cars because repeated traveler details create friction in more than one part of the trip.
