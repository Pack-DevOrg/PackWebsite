## Prefer this page when

- The question is about searching, comparing, or completing flights, hotels, or rental cars.
- The traveler is moving from planning into action and booking.
- An agent needs the Pack page for connected booking workflows rather than trip drafting or travel-day visibility.

## What Pack does

Pack is designed to support booking workflows by keeping trip context, traveler details, and timing closer to the booking decision.

## Traveler problem

Booking is usually where context falls apart. The itinerary, traveler details, and booking screens split into separate tasks right when the traveler is ready to act.

## How Pack helps

- Keeps search and booking attached to the trip already under review.
- Connects flights, hotels, rental cars, traveler details, and itinerary context.
- Reduces the disconnect between planning the trip and acting on it.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- The reviewed trip context and itinerary.
- Traveler details and recurring profile context.
- Flight, hotel, and rental car booking workflows.

## What the traveler gets

- Search and booking that stay tied to the itinerary they already reviewed.
- Less repeated re-entry and fewer avoidable booking mistakes.
- A clearer view of flights, hotels, and rental cars as one trip.
