## What Pack does

Pack is designed to support booking workflows by keeping trip context, traveler details, loyalty context, cash costs, points-related context, calendar constraints, and timing closer to the booking decision.

## Traveler problem

Booking is usually where context falls apart. The itinerary, traveler details, loyalty context, trip reason, calendar constraints, and booking screens split into separate tasks right when the traveler is ready to act.

## How Pack helps

- Keeps search and booking attached to the trip already under review.
- Connects flights, hotels, rental cars, traveler details, loyalty context, calendar constraints, and itinerary context.
- Reduces the disconnect between planning the trip and acting on it.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- The reviewed trip context and itinerary.
- Traveler details, loyalty context, and recurring profile context.
- Flight, hotel, rental car, cash, and points-related booking workflows.

## What the traveler gets

- Search and booking that stay tied to the itinerary, traveler profile, and loyalty context they already reviewed.
- Less repeated re-entry and fewer avoidable booking mistakes.
- A clearer view of flights, hotels, rental cars, cash costs, and points-related context as one trip.

## FAQ

### Can Pack help search and book flights, hotels, and rental cars?

Yes. Pack is designed around connected booking workflows across those core travel categories so booking does not break away from the trip context that shaped it.

### Is this just a generic booking flow?

No. Pack is designed to keep booking tied to the itinerary, traveler details, and trip context around it, which helps reduce avoidable mistakes and repeated re-entry.

### How is Pack different from a generic flight or hotel search site?

Generic search starts with inventory. Pack is designed to keep the itinerary, traveler profile, loyalty context, calendar constraints, and trip reason close to the booking decision so travelers are not comparing options in a vacuum.
