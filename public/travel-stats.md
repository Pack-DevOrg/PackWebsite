## FAQ

### Can Pack show trips on a map?

Yes. Pack is designed to show travel maps and trip context across travel records so places, timing, and movement are easier to review instead of staying buried inside separate bookings.

### Are these stats only about flights?

No. The point is broader trip context across flights, hotels, rental cars, and related details so the trip is easier to understand as one experience.
