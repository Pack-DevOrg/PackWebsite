## FAQ

### Can Pack help manage traveler profiles and preferences?

Yes. Pack is designed to organize traveler context such as preferences, loyalty information, accessibility needs, and trusted traveler details so repeat bookings feel less repetitive.

### How are traveler profiles different from travel history?

Travel history is the record of where the traveler has been. Traveler profiles hold the recurring details that shape future trips and save re-entry later.

### Can traveler profiles help with airline points and loyalty programs?

Yes. Traveler profiles can keep loyalty numbers, program context, trusted traveler details, and preferences close to planning and booking, which is different from a standalone points balance tracker because the information stays tied to the trip workflow.
