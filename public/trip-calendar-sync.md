## FAQ

### Can Pack sync trip events to a calendar?

Yes. Pack is designed to sync trip events back to device calendars so travel timing stays visible in the schedule people already check throughout the day.

### Why is this different from connected accounts?

Connected accounts bring context in. Calendar sync pushes useful trip timing back out so the traveler does not have to manually copy itinerary details into the schedule they already use.
