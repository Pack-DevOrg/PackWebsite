## What Pack does

Pack can help keep trip expenses, group trip costs, shared travel costs, and related booking context easier to review together.

## Traveler problem

After a trip, the itinerary often lives in one place while the real cost of the trip is scattered across receipts, notes, spreadsheets, reimbursements, and group expense conversations.

## How Pack helps

- Keeps expenses attached to the trip instead of separate notes or spreadsheets.
- Makes it easier to review the full cost picture later.
- Supports future planning with a better memory of what travel really cost and how costs changed across travelers.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- Trip expenses and related cost records.
- Flights, hotels, rental cars, shared plans, booking context, and the broader trip record.

## What the traveler gets

- Trip costs that stay attached to the itinerary.
- An easier way to review the full cost of travel later.
- Better memory of what trips actually cost when planning again or coordinating a group.

## FAQ

### Can Pack help organize travel expenses?

Yes. Pack is designed to keep trip expenses organized as part of the broader trip record so the cost of travel does not get separated from the trip itself.

### Why not leave expenses in a separate tool?

Because the cost of a trip is easier to understand when it stays attached to the itinerary and travel history around it instead of being reconstructed later from separate notes and reports.

### Can Pack help with group trip costs?

Pack is designed to keep trip expenses attached to the itinerary and shared trip context, which makes group travel costs easier to understand than when receipts, notes, and reimbursements live separately.
