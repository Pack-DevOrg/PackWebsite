## What Pack does

Pack can plan trips proactively from public events, private email and calendar events, and plain-language prompts.

## Traveler problem

Real trips often begin before anyone opens a booking site. A meeting gets added, an event appears, or a rough idea becomes urgent, and then the traveler has to translate that context into bookings by hand.

## How Pack helps

- Starts from the reason the trip exists.
- Turns events, timing, and prompts into an earlier travel draft.
- Keeps schedule context closer to itinerary planning before search gets fragmented.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- Public events and travel-worthy dates.
- Private email and calendar timing.
- Plain-language prompts such as destination ideas or client travel requests.

## What the traveler gets

- A trip that can take shape earlier while the reason for travel is still clear.
- Less manual translation from events into booked travel.
- Timing constraints that stay attached to the trip draft.

## FAQ

### Can Pack help plan a trip from a calendar or event?

Yes. Pack is designed to use event and timing context to help assemble a trip draft earlier, which is useful when the reason for travel already exists but the itinerary has not been built yet.

### Can a trip start from a plain-language prompt?

Yes. Pack is built to work from prompts as well as travel data and scheduling context so the traveler can start from what they know instead of waiting until every detail is structured.
