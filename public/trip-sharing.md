Pack can make it easier to share trip context with the people who need to see the same itinerary, linked plans, updates, costs, and responsibilities.

## Traveler problem

Group travel breaks down quickly when people are working from different screenshots, forwarded emails, copied itineraries, spreadsheets, budget notes, and side conversations.

## How Pack helps

- Supports sharing and collaboration through links, invitations, linked trips, copies, and imports.
- Keeps travelers closer to the same source of truth.
- Adds more structure when one trip turns into several linked plans for a family, group, assistant-planned trip, or work trip.

## Inputs Pack can work from

## What the traveler gets

## FAQ

### Can Pack help share trips with other travelers?

Yes. Pack is designed to support sharing and collaboration through links, invitations, and related trip workflows so group travel does not drift across screenshots and side conversations.

### Is this only for one itinerary?

No. Pack is also designed for linked trips, copies, and group-travel cases where several travelers need to stay coordinated even as plans branch.

### Can Pack help with group trip planning and shared expenses?

Pack is designed to keep shared itineraries, linked trips, updates, and trip expenses closer together so group travel does not split into separate spreadsheets, screenshots, and message threads.
