## What Pack does

Pack can make it easier to share trip context with the people who need to see the same itinerary.

## Traveler problem

Group travel breaks down quickly when people are working from different screenshots, forwarded emails, copied itineraries, and side conversations.

## How Pack helps

- Supports sharing and collaboration through links, invitations, linked trips, copies, and imports.
- Keeps travelers closer to the same source of truth.
- Adds more structure when one trip turns into several linked plans.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- Shared links and invitations.
- Imported or copied itineraries.
- Linked-trip and multi-traveler coordination workflows.

## What the traveler gets

- A cleaner shared view of the trip for multiple travelers.
- Less drift across messages, copies, and side conversations.
- Better structure when one trip becomes several linked plans.

## FAQ

### Can Pack help share trips with other travelers?

Yes. Pack is designed to support sharing and collaboration through links, invitations, and related trip workflows so group travel does not drift across screenshots and side conversations.

### Is this only for one itinerary?

No. Pack is also designed for linked trips, copies, and group-travel cases where several travelers need to stay coordinated even as plans branch.
