## What Pack does

Pack is designed to keep trips editable from chat, voice, inbox updates, photos, calendar changes, and related travel inputs.

## Traveler problem

Trip changes do not happen in one clean workflow. They show up across messages, emails, calendars, screenshots, and quick conversations, which makes itinerary cleanup messy and easy to miss.

## How Pack helps

- Treats updates as a cross-channel problem instead of a single-screen problem.
- Keeps trip changes connected to one organized record.
- Reduces manual itinerary cleanup after changes happen elsewhere.

## Inputs Pack can work from

- Chat and voice changes.
- Email and calendar updates.
- Photos and metadata that preserve or reattach trip context.

## What the traveler gets

- One organized trip record that can keep up with changing plans.
- Less manual cleanup after updates happen in other channels.
- Trip edits that feel closer to conversation than admin work.

## FAQ

### Can Pack help update a trip from chat or voice?

Yes. Pack is designed to let travel changes stay connected to the organized trip even when they begin in conversation, which helps when updates happen too quickly for manual itinerary cleanup.

### Can email, calendar, and photos affect trip updates?

Yes. Those signals can help inform how a trip is reorganized or refreshed inside Pack so the itinerary does not drift away from what changed elsewhere.
