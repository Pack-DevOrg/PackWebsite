## FAQ

### Can Pack show details that matter right before a trip?

Yes. Pack is designed to surface upcoming trip context such as timing, weather, airport signals, and related actions so the traveler does not have to check several tools right before departure.

### Does this include airport security information?

It can. Pack's upcoming trip view is designed to work alongside airport security-wait information and other travel-day signals so departure-day decisions are easier to make in context.

### Can Pack combine flight alerts, airport waits, weather, and drive time?

Pack is designed around that combined travel-day readiness problem: the useful answer is not only a flight alert or a wait-time number, but how timing, airport conditions, weather, transportation, and the itinerary fit together.
