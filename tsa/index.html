How this board works

The useful part should be the wait times. These details exist so travelers, answer engines, and assistants can understand where the data comes from, how fresh it is, and where to go when an airport only publishes a manual page.

Pack ❤️ travelers. We want to give people the best travel experience we possibly can, and making airport wait information easier to find and easier to use is one small part of that.
